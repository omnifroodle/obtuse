There are two ways of financing a project. One is by making a loan to an entrepreneur; the other is by buying a share in the enterprise. The first is usury and wicked. The second is partnership and honourable.
It is evil to take interest on loans.
Five ways to use your intellect: (1) withholding judgement, (2) tentative assent, allowing that one could be wrong, (3) unquestioning assent on the basis of self-evidence, (4) unquestioning assent on the basis of reasons (scientia), (5) unquestioning assent where there are no compelling reasons (faith). 
Thinking is the production of inner sentences.
